# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Engaged couples, wedding guests, and wedding planners using Tu dia de blanco to present wedding information, collect details, and manage event moments with a calm, polished experience. Couples arrive with high emotional investment and limited time; guests need clarity and reassurance without feeling like they are using a corporate tool.

## Product Purpose

Tu dia de blanco creates elegant digital wedding experiences: public wedding pages, guided inquiry flows, guest-facing tools, and private management screens. Success means the product feels personal and memorable on the public surface, while still making operational tasks feel simple and composed behind the scenes.

## Brand Personality

Warm, editorial, minimalist. The tone should feel intimate and carefully hosted, with enough restraint to let photography, typography, and meaningful copy carry the experience.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS login screens, heavy luxury cliches, cluttered wedding decoration, stock-looking beige visuals, loud gradients, glassy panels, and interfaces that feel like templates instead of a considered wedding brand.

## Design Principles

1. Let wedding imagery feel physical and tactile, not decorative.
2. Prefer editorial restraint over ornamental excess.
3. Make every path back to the public brand feel intentional.
4. Keep management surfaces quiet, legible, and efficient.
5. Use copy sparingly, like a host leaving a thoughtful note.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use WCAG AA as the baseline. Preserve keyboard navigation, visible focus states, sufficient contrast, readable type sizes, and reduced-motion compatibility. Avoid relying on color alone for state or meaning.
